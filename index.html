About Vega Labs

Located in Juan R. Liwag Memorial High School, Vega Labs is an innovative hub where 8th-grade students explore the wonders of science. Through hands-on experiments, creative projects, and state-of-the-art equipment like microscopes and telescopes, students engage in activities that spark curiosity and foster critical thinking. From chemistry experiments and physics projects to biology studies, Vega Labs bridges theory and real-world application, empowering young minds to innovate, collaborate, and discover.

Meet the Owner

Vega Labs is proudly managed by Lorna P. Vega. A teacher from JRLMHS with a passion for teaching. The lab is owned by the school and she manages the lab herself. She aims to inspire students by providing them with the tools and opportunities to explore the wonders of the scientific world.
